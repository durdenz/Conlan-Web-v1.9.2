Property Features

Conlan Professional Center - The perfect location in Palm Bay. One of Florida's fastest growing communities. Offering Retail Space, Private Executive Office Suites, and Individual Executive Offices from 100 to 1,714 SqFt.

Unparalleled Visibility

The Plaza with prime four lane footage on both streets, incorporating stores and offices ranging in size from 1,078 to 1,804 square feet. Conlan Professional Center also offers one of the area's nicest Private Executive Office Suites and Individual Executive Offices, from 100 to 1,714 square feet across 2 floors, with deluxe elevator service and individual, uniform tenant identification signage.

Conlan Professional Center also offers Individual Executive Offices for those that need an Individual Executive Office but not an entire Suite.

Set Your Sites In A Strategic Location

Conlan Professional Center is ideally located: 25,500 square feet of prime space at one of Brevard County's busiest intersections - with traffic volume of greater than 10,000 vehicles per day. The site includes convenient access to Palm Bay Road and Robert J Conlan Boulevard. These two roads offer direct access to both US1 and I-95. Ample onsite parking is available and the building elevations incorporate contemporary tenant and project graphics.

The Growth Center For Professional And High Technology Employment

Palm Bay is the second largest city in Brevard County, and the heart of the Florida space industry. Palm Bay and Melbourne make up the majority of South Brevard County which is the ninth fastest growing metropolitan region in the US. Overall, Brevard is ranked seventh in population of 67 counties in Florida. Colan Professional Center boasts a population of 119,000+ in it's immediate vicinity. Perhaps the strongest identity for Palm Bay is the presence of Florida's largest private employer, the L3Harris Corporation, with 12,000 employees in the area. Two of these facilities are located across the street from Conlan Professional Center.
